# CarLens Vision

## Mission

CarLens helps anyone instantly identify and learn about any vehicle using AI.

Users can simply take a photo of a car and instantly receive:
- Vehicle identification
- Specifications
- Reliability information
- Common issues
- Estimated market value
- AI-powered buying advice

---

## Target Audience

- Used car buyers
- First-time drivers
- Car enthusiasts
- Dealership customers
- Marketplace shoppers

---

## Problem

Buying or identifying a car often requires searching multiple websites for information.

CarLens solves this by bringing everything into one app with a single photo.

---

## Long-Term Vision

Become the smartest AI companion for car ownership and car shopping.

Eventually CarLens will help users:

- Buy cars
- Sell cars
- Maintain cars
- Understand repairs
- Estimate values
- Detect damage
- Learn about any vehicle instantly
