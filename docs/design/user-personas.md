# User Personas

## Persona 1 — First-Time Buyer

### Name
Alex

### Age
22

### Goal
Buy a reliable used car without getting ripped off.

### Frustrations

- Doesn't know much about cars
- Doesn't know what's a fair price
- Doesn't know common problems

### How CarLens Helps

Alex scans a car at a dealership.

Within seconds CarLens explains:

- What the car is
- Whether it's reliable
- What issues to inspect
- Whether the asking price is reasonable
